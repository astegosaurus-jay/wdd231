WDD 231 Nostalgic Reflection site plan - Jason Jimenez

Site Name: Nostalgic Reflection

The reason I choose this name is because I want to look back at the hobbies and passions I had growing up and reflect on how they made me who I am today and why I fell in love with them in the first place

Site purpose

Countinuing off of the prior paragraph, I want to archive some of the things I had growing up and reflect on its impact on me. I think it is so easy now to lose that joy in so many hobbies now and I want to reflect on the joy of it. This will also be a way to journal for the first time for me and hopefully help encourage people to look back at their passions and hobbies themselves

Scenarios

I am mainly doing it for myself and my family and freinds. Also for people who feel the same about their hobbies. One example would be how can I reflect on my passions. How can I share my memories with the ones I love.

Color

I will use purple the headers. I will use a grey for the background colors

Typography

I will use cabin font for the headers. I will use nata sans on paragraphs.
